# Easylearn Competitor Validation: Executive Summary

**Research Objective:** Validate opportunity to build a competitor to easylearn.ch  
**Method:** 9 qualitative depth interviews (90 min each)  
**Date:** 2026-02-27  
**Analyst:** GrowthAgent

---

## TL;DR Verdict

| Segment | Verdict | Why |
|---------|---------|-----|
| Small companies (10-50) with price-sensitive users | **GREEN LIGHT** | Low switching cost, fast decisions, high dissatisfaction |
| Mid-size (50-200) first-time buyers | **CAUTIOUS** | Need education + pilot, 3-6 month cycle |
| Enterprise (500+) experienced buyers | **RED LIGHT (for now)** | 12-18 month cycle, needs 10+ references first |
| Individual prosumer market | **YELLOW LIGHT** | Different product entirely - modern learning hub vs LMS |

## Findings by Dimension

### 1. Price Sensitivity Variations

| Segment | Price Elasticity | Optimal Price Point | Key Insight |
|---------|-----------------|---------------------|-------------|
| **High Sensitivity** (Small startup, first LMS) | Very High | **CHF 5-8/user/month** | Anchored to Netflix/Spotify pricing. "CHF 15 is my gym membership" |
| **Medium Sensitivity** (Mid-size, cost-conscious) | Medium | **CHF 10-15/user/month** | ROI calculation: CHF 11K/year vs CHF 15-20K savings on external training |
| **Low Sensitivity** (Enterprise, experienced) | Low | **CHF 15-20/user/month** | Evaluates TCO, not unit price. Price is #7 priority after compliance, integration, risk |
| **Individual/Prosumer** | Very High | **CHF 20-30/month total** | Compares to MasterClass (CHF 180/year), Skillshare (CHF 15/mo) |

**Quote (High sensitivity):** *"CHF 15 per month? That's almost my gym membership. For a website that feels like my university portal from 2015?"*

**Quote (Low sensitivity):** *"My CFO doesn't blink at CHF 50,000/year for compliance training. He blinks at audit findings."*

---

### 2. Organization Size Variations

| Size | Decision Speed | Sales Model | Migration Risk | Strategic Implication |
|------|---------------|-------------|----------------|----------------------|
| **10-50 employees** | Days/weeks | Self-serve PLG | None (greenfield) | **Ideal entry point** - founder decides, no procurement |
| **50-200 employees** | Weeks/months | Inside sales + demo | Low | Education-based sales, need case studies |
| **200-500 employees** | Months | Field sales + pilot | Medium | Need 3+ Swiss references first |
| **500+ employees** | 6-12 months | Enterprise sales + RFP | High | **Avoid until enterprise-ready** |

**Key insight:** Small companies (10-50) are the perfect wedge - no migration burden, no compliance complexity (yet), founder makes snap decisions, and price sensitivity drives them toward disruptors.

**Quote (Small org):** *"He'll try it for a month and decide if it's worth it"* (12-person company, founder decides)

**Quote (Enterprise):** *"Switching costs me 6 months of my life. The juice has to be worth the squeeze."* (600-person company, 6+ approval chain)

### 3. Experience Level Variations

| Experience | Evaluation Criteria | Main Friction | Sales Strategy |
|------------|-------------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Novice** (First LMS) | Trust, simplicity, price | Fear of wrong choice | Education, free trial, comparison guides |
| **Intermediate** (1-2 platforms) | Features, UX, ROI | Migration concerns | Demo, pilot program, case studies |
| **Expert** (3+ platforms, 8+ years) | Integration, compliance, TCO | Stakeholder management | Consultative sales, custom solutions, references |

**Novice behavior:** *"What's SCORM? Do I need it?"* - Learning as they go, needs hand-holding

**Expert behavior:** *"I can tell you exactly what easylearn does poorly - and also why we're not switching this year."* - Sophisticated evaluator, knows workarounds, high negotiation leverage

## Strategic Recommendations

### Recommended Entry Strategy

```
Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Bottom-Up Wedge
├── Target: 10-50 employee companies
├── Price: CHF 6-8/user/month (freemium entry)
├── Sales: Self-serve + chat support
└── Goal: 50 companies, case studies

Phase 2 (Months 6-12): Land-and-Expand
├── Target: 50-200 employee companies
├── Price: CHF 10-15/user/month
├── Sales: Inside sales + pilots
└── Goal: 10 reference customers, compliance parity

Phase 3 (Months 12-24): Enterprise (if desired)
├── Target: 200-500+ employee companies
├── Price: CHF 15-20/user/month
├── Sales: Enterprise team, RFP responses
└── Goal: 3-5 enterprise logos
```

### Critical Success Factors

| Factor | Requirement | Risk if Missing |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| Swiss data hosting | Non-negotiable | Immediate disqualification |
| Compliance reporting | Match easylearn audit capabilities | Can't sell to regulated industries |
| Modern UX | 10x better than easylearn | Users won't advocate for switch |
| Migration service | Full-service, not DIY tools | Buyers won't risk switch |
| References | 3+ Swiss customers at target size | Can't sell to experienced buyers |

### Pricing Architecture Recommendation

| Tier | Price | Target | Features |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| **Starter** | CHF 6/user/mo | 10-50 employees | Core LMS, basic content, email support |
| **Growth** | CHF 12/user/mo | 50-200 employees | Advanced reporting, integrations, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | CHF 18/user/mo | 200+ employees | Custom contracts, SSO, API, dedicated CSM |
| **Freemium** | Free (5 users) | Evaluation | Limited courses, basic features |

### Key Risks

1. **Switching costs are real** - Even dissatisfied users stay because migration is painful
2. **Trust gap** - New vendor vs. 5-10 year incumbent with Swiss compliance track record
3. **Compliance complexity** - Swiss labor law, data protection, FINMA readiness (for banks)
4. **Feature parity expectation** - Buyers expect full LMS day 1, not MVP
5. **Sales cycle mismatch** - Trying to sell enterprise features to small companies, or vice versa

## Alternative Path: Adjacent Category

If targeting Non-Users (Persona 3), build a **different product entirely**:

| | Traditional LMS (Easylearn competitor) | Modern Learning Hub (Adjacent category) |
|--|----------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| **Primary user** | HR / L&D manager | Individual learner |
| **Purchase driver** | Compliance, cost reduction | Career growth, skill acquisition |
| **Content model** | Pre-built course catalogs | Just-in-time microlearning |
| **Social element** | Assigned training paths | Peer learning, cohorts, mentorship |
| **Price model** | B2B per-user SaaS | B2C subscription + B2B team add-on |
| **Key feature** | Audit reporting | Learning stipend management |
| **Go-to-market** | Sales-led | Community + content marketing |

**Verdict:** These are two different businesses. Choose one.
