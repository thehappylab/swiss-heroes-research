Executive Summary

easylearn is a Swiss e-learning platform provider for corporate training. They offer an integrated LMS (Learning Management System), e-learning content, event management, and content authoring tools. A classic "build once, sell many times" SaaS business - perfect for small team operation.
Disruption Potential: HIGH - Traditional LMS with dated UX, ripe for modern AI-powered learning platform.

Disruption Strategy

AI-Generated Course Creation

Their weakness: Manual content production
Your play:

• Upload PDF → auto-convert to interactive course
• AI voiceover generation
• Auto-quiz generation from content
• 10x faster content production

Microlearning Mobile-First

Their weakness: Desktop-heavy traditional LMS
Your play:

• 5-minute daily learning modules
• Mobile app with push notifications
• TikTok-style video learning
• Gamification with streaks/badges
Compliance Automation

Their weakness: Generic platform
Your play:

• Swiss-specific compliance (ArG, data protection)
• Auto-assigned training based on role
• Certification tracking & reminders
• Audit-ready reporting

Freemium + Self-Service

Their weakness: Demo → sales call → implementation
Your play:

• Free tier (5 users, basic courses)
• Credit card signup (no sales call)
• Instant deployment
• Upgrade in-app
