## Executive Summary

Magic Heidi is the "only Swiss accounting software native on iPhone, Android, Mac, Windows, AND Web." ## Disruption Strategy

### 1. Industry-Specific Versions
**Their weakness:** Generic freelancer focus  
**Your play:**
- "Magic Designer" - For graphic designers
- "Magic Developer" - For software devs
- "Magic Consultant" - For business consultants
- Industry-specific templates and categories

### 2. Embedded Tax Advising
**Their weakness:** Software only  
**Your play:**
- AI tax optimization suggestions
- Direct connection to fiduciaries
- "Review my taxes" service
- CHF 99 annual tax review

### 3. Freelancer Community
**Their weakness:** Solo tool  
**Your play:**
- Community features within app
- "Find other freelancers" directory
- Collaboration tools
- Shared project billing

### 4. Predictive Business Insights
**Their weakness:** Historical data only  
**Your play:**
- "Your slow months are coming"
- Client profitability scoring
- Optimal pricing suggestions
- Benchmark vs. similar freelancers
