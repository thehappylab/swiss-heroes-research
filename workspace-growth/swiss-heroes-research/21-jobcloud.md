## Executive Summary

JobCloud is Switzerland's dominant recruitment platform, operating jobs.ch and jobup.ch. With Ringier backing and 60%+ market share, they're the gatekeepers of Swiss online recruitment. However, niche and vertical job platforms are eating at their edges.

**Disruption Potential: MEDIUM** - Hard to compete head-on, but vertical specialization works.

### Revenue Streams
1. **Job Postings** - CHF 300-800 per post

## Disruption Strategy

### 1. Vertical Job Platforms
**Their weakness:** Generalist approach  
**Your play:**
- Fintechjobs.ch - Only fintech roles
- Greentechjobs.ch - Sustainability sector
- Cryptojobs.ch - Blockchain/web3
- AI jobs aggregator - ML/AI specific

### 2. Passive Candidate Sourcing
**Their weakness:** Active job seekers only  
**Your play:**
- LinkedIn outreach automation
- Talent pool building
- "We'll find you" service
- CHF 2,000 per placement

### 3. Video-First Job Platform
**Their weakness:** Text-based job ads  
**Your play:**
- 60-second job videos
- Culture video tours
- Manager intro videos
- TikTok-style job discovery

### 4. Freelancer Marketplace
**Their weakness:** Permanent jobs focus  
**Your play:**
- Freelancer project matching
- Gig economy for professionals
- CHF 50-200/project fee
- Focus on creative/tech roles
