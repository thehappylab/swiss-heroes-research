## Disruption Strategy

### 1. SME Self-Service Surveys
**Their weakness:** Enterprise complexity  
**Your play:**
- CHF 49/month flat rate
- Pre-built templates (NPS, CSAT, employee)
- No implementation needed
- Credit card signup

### 2. AI Survey Generation
**Their weakness:** Manual survey building  
**Your play:**
- "I want to measure customer satisfaction" → AI generates survey
- Smart question suggestions
- Automatic translation (DE/FR/IT/EN)
- Optimal length calculation

### 3. WhatsApp/SMS Surveys
**Their weakness:** Email/web focus  
**Your play:**
- WhatsApp Business integration
- SMS surveys for high response rates
- QR code surveys (restaurants, events)
- In-app feedback SDK

### 4. Industry-Specific Bundles
**Their weakness:** Generic platform  
**Your play:**
- "Restaurant Package" - Menu feedback, service ratings
- "Retail Package" - Store experience, NPS
- "SaaS Package" - Product feedback, churn prediction
- Pre-configured for vertical
